Blue Lock es un manga japonés de fútbol escrito por Muneyuki Kaneshiro
e ilustrado por Yusuke Nomura.La historia sigue a un equipo de fútbol
juvenil que se forma para crear el delantero perfecto.

The manga explores themes of competition, teamwork, and personal growth as Isagi and his fellow participants strive to stand out and earn a spot on the national team. "Blue Lock" gained popularity for its dynamic soccer action scenes and character development. It has also been adapted into various media, including a mobile game and an anime series.
